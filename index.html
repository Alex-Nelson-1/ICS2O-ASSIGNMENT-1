About Larry Page


What did Larry Page do?


Larry page is a famous American computer scientist for creating Google, along with Sergey Brin, a search enging that has become one of the most popular search engines today. The first search engine that Brin and Page, ever made was called "Backrub". The code that Larry Page and Sergey Brin wrote for Backrub, dubbed PageRank, revolutionized the way search engines were made. PageRank took into account the number of pages, as well as the importane of the pages, whereas every other engine ranked sites by how often a search term appeared. The search engine "Google" has become the most popular search engine to date by far, with an estimated 91% of searches going through Google. After making making a load of money through Google, Larry decided to invest in start ups for flying cars.
